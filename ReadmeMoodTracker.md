# 🌈 Mood Tracker

A beautiful, lightweight daily mood tracking application with calendar heatmap visualization and detailed statistics. Track your emotions, discover patterns, and improve your mental well-being.

![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)
![Language](https://img.shields.io/badge/language-JavaScript-yellow.svg)
![No Dependencies](https://img.shields.io/badge/dependencies-none-brightgreen.svg)

## ✨ Features

- 📅 **Calendar Heatmap** — Visual representation of your mood throughout the month with color-coded days
- 😊 **5-Level Mood Scale** — Express how you feel with intuitive emoji buttons (Terrible to Great)
- 📝 **Daily Notes** — Add optional notes to remember what influenced your mood
- 📊 **Statistics Dashboard** — Track total entries, average mood, current streak, and best days
- 📈 **Mood Distribution Chart** — Visualize the proportion of different moods over time
- 🇺🇦 **Bilingual Support** — Full Ukrainian (default) and English translations
- 💾 **Local Storage** — All data persists between sessions, no account required
- 📱 **Responsive Design** — Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- 🔄 **Orientation Support** — Optimized layouts for both portrait and landscape modes
- 🎨 **Beautiful UI** — Modern glassmorphism design with smooth gradients and animations
- 🚀 **Zero Dependencies** — Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
- 🔒 **Privacy First** — All data stays on your device, nothing is sent to any server

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Online

Visit the live application at [moodtrackerjs.github.io](https://moodtrackerjs.github.io)

### Local Installation

Clone the repository and open `index.html` in your browser:

```bash
git clone https://github.com/moodtrackerjs/moodtrackerjs.github.io.git
cd moodtrackerjs.github.io
```

Then simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser. No build process, no server, no installation required.

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Log Your Mood** — Click one of the five emoji buttons reflecting your current mood
2. **Add a Note** *(optional)* — Write a brief note about your day or what's influencing your mood
3. **Save** — Click the "Save mood" button to record your entry
4. **Track Progress** — View your mood history on the calendar heatmap
5. **Explore Statistics** — Check your streaks, averages, and mood distribution
6. **Switch Language** — Toggle between Ukrainian (УКР) and English (ENG) anytime

### Mood Scale

| Emoji | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 😢 | Terrible |
| 😕 | Bad |
| 😐 | Okay |
| 🙂 | Good |
| 😄 | Great |

### Statistics Explained

- **Total Entries** — Number of days you've logged your mood
- **Average Mood** — Your overall mood score (1.0 to 5.0)
- **Current Streak** — Consecutive days of mood logging
- **Best Day** — Highest mood rating you've recorded

## 🌍 Localization

The application supports two languages out of the box:

- 🇺🇦 **Ukrainian** (default)
- 🇬🇧 **English**

Language preference is saved to local storage and restored on subsequent visits. To add additional languages, extend the `translations` object in the JavaScript section.
