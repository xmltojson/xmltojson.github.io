🎨 Multiple Themes — Animals, Food, Nature & Space emoji sets

⏸️ Pause & Resume — Pick up right where you left off
🏆 High Score Leaderboard — Top 5 scores saved locally
🎊 Confetti Celebration — Dazzling win animations

✓ CSS 3D card flip animations with perspective transforms

✓ LocalStorage persistence — game state & high scores saved

✓ Animated starry background with floating particles
✓ Smooth cubic-bezier transitions & keyframe animations
✓ Accessibility-friendly design
