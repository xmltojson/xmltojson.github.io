We're thrilled to bring you another update to **ШвидкоПереклад** — the free AI translator built for everyone navigating life across different languages! 🌍

This release puts **the power of a personal phrasebook in your hands**. Now you can create your own phrases and sections — and let the app translate them into every supported language with a single click! ➕✨

🎁 **WHAT'S NEW IN v7.0:**

➕ **Custom Phrases** — Add your own phrases to any built-in or personal section
📁 **Custom Sections** — Organize phrases into your own categories, complete with emoji icons
✨ **One-Click Auto-Translate** — Type a phrase once, hit "Перекласти решту," and watch all five languages fill instantly
🧠 **Smart Fill** — Only empty fields are populated, so your manual edits are always preserved
⌨️ **Enter Shortcut** — Press Enter in the source field to trigger prefill without reaching for the mouse
🏷️ **Custom Badges** — Your own phrases are clearly marked with a "Власна" label
🗑️ **Easy Cleanup** — Delete individual phrases or entire sections with a tap
💾 **Backup-Ready** — Custom phrases and sections are included in your ZIP archives automatically

🖥️ **TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS:**

✓ Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no build step, no framework
✓ Auto-translate prefill reuses the multi-provider fallback chain (Google → MyMemory → LibreTranslate)
✓ Custom modal system upgraded with async validation and full form support
✓ Field-level flash animation confirms which translations were auto-filled
✓ Counts as a single translation-quota use, no matter how many languages fill
✓ Custom phrases & sections persisted via LocalStorage and bundled into ZIP backups
✓ Fully responsive — the add-phrase form adapts gracefully to mobile screens
✓ Ukrainian-first interface that stays warm, clear, and friendly throughout
