🎁 **WHAT'S NEW IN v5.0:**

💾 **Backup to ZIP Archive** — Export everything into one portable file with a single tap
📥 **One-Click Restore** — Bring back all your data on any device, instantly
🗂️ **Complete Scan Backup** — Your full-resolution images travel inside the archive too
📁 **Smart Naming** — Archives auto-named `shvedkopereklad_YYMMDDHHMM.zip` for easy sorting
🏳️ **Crisp SVG Flags** — Language flags now render perfectly on every OS & browser
🔒 **100% Offline Backups** — Your archive is generated locally and never uploaded anywhere
🛡️ **Safe Restore Flow** — Clear confirmation before any data is replaced
🔄 **Seamless Migration** — Switch phones or browsers without losing a single translation

🖥️ **TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS:**

✓ Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no build step, no framework
✓ JSZip-powered archive with DEFLATE compression
✓ Full data bundle: settings, history, saved items, current photo & all scans
✓ Image blobs encoded and packaged inside the ZIP for complete portability
✓ IndexedDB v2 with dedicated `scans` object store & time-indexed queries
✓ Inline SVG flags replace emoji for consistent cross-platform rendering
✓ Automatic manifest & versioning for future-proof restores
✓ 100% client-side — your documents never leave your device
