❓ How to Play

🎯 Goal

Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as possible. Your speed is measured in CPM (Characters Per Minute) and WPM (Words Per Minute).

📝 Instructions

  • Select your preferred language
  • Choose a time duration (30s, 60s, or 120s)
  • Press Enter or click "Start Test"
  • Type the text shown in the display area
  • Complete each text to move to the next one
  • Test ends when timer runs out

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Start Test Enter
  • Reset / Close Modal Esc
  • Try Again (after test) Tab
  • Open Help F1
  • Typing Test Tab 1
  • Calendar Tab 2
  • Statistics Tab 3
  • Achievements Tab 4
  • English Alt + 1
  • Ukrainian Alt + 2
  • Russian Alt + 3
  • German Alt + 4

⭐ Scoring System

  • ⭐⭐⭐ Three Stars 95%+ accuracy & 200+ CPM
  • ⭐⭐ Two Stars 85%+ accuracy & 150+ CPM
  • ⭐ One Star Complete the test

🔥 Streaks & Bonuses

Visit daily to build your streak! Each consecutive day earns you bonus points:

  • Daily visit bonus Streak × 10 points
  • Daily star +1 ⭐

💡 Pro Tip: Focus on accuracy first, then speed. Complete each text to move to the next one and maximize your score!
