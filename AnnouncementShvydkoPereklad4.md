Dear Community,

We're thrilled to announce another major update to **ШвидкоПереклад** — the free AI translator built for everyone navigating life across different languages! 🌍

This release focuses on one thing you asked for again and again: **never lose your scanned documents**. Now every photo you translate is safely stored, searchable, and ready whenever you need it. 📷✨

🎁 **WHAT'S NEW IN v4.0:**

🗂️ **Scan History Tab** — Every OCR scan is automatically saved (up to 100 entries)
🖼️ **Image Thumbnails** — Visual previews of every document you've scanned
🔍 **Search Everywhere** — Instantly find any translation in History, Saved & Scans
✨ **Highlighted Matches** — Search terms glow in gold so you spot them at a glance
🕐 **Human-Friendly Dates** — "щойно", "5 хв тому", "вчора о 14:30" instead of raw timestamps
👁️ **Full Scan Viewer** — Tap any thumbnail to see the full image, text & translation
📋 **Per-Scan Actions** — View, Copy, Speak, Delete right from the scan list
🗑️ **Granular Clearing** — Wipe History, Saved, or Scans independently — or everything at once

🖥️ **TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS:**

✓ Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — Zero dependencies (except Tesseract.js for OCR)
✓ IndexedDB v2 with dedicated `scans` object store & time-indexed queries
✓ Canvas-based thumbnail generation (160px JPEG @ 70% quality)
✓ Automatic store trimming keeps only your 100 newest scans
✓ Full-resolution image blobs stored locally, thumbnails cached for speed
✓ Live search filtering with regex-safe highlight rendering
✓ Smart relative date formatting in Ukrainian (seconds → years)
✓ 100% client-side — your documents never leave your device
