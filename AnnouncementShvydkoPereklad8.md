We're thrilled to bring you a landmark update to **ШвидкоПереклад** — the free AI translator built for everyone navigating life across different languages! 🌍

This release transforms your device into a **real-time interpreter**. Now you can simply speak in one language and hear the translation spoken back to you automatically — enabling natural, hands-free conversations! 🗣️✨

🎁 **WHAT'S NEW IN v8.0:**

🗣️ **Live Speech-to-Speech** — A brand-new "Розмова" tab that listens, translates, and speaks the result automatically
🎙️ **Tap-to-Talk Interface** — A large, friendly microphone button with clear visual states: idle, listening, and processing
📝 **Live Transcription** — Watch your words appear on screen as you speak, with interim results shown in real-time
🔁 **Continuous Mode** — After speaking the translation, the app automatically resumes listening for your next phrase — perfect for back-and-forth dialogue
🔊 **Auto-Speak Toggle** — Turn automatic voice output on or off with a single tap
⇄ **Instant Language Swap** — Flip source and target languages on the fly mid-conversation
💬 **Conversation Log** — Every exchange is recorded with per-item replay and copy actions
🔂 **Replay Button** — Hear the last translation again anytime you need it

🖥️ **TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS:**

✓ Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no build step, no framework
✓ Full pipeline: Web Speech recognition → multi-provider translation → text-to-speech
✓ Reuses the trusted fallback chain (Google → MyMemory → LibreTranslate)
✓ Enhanced TTS engine with an `onDone` callback so listening resumes seamlessly after speech ends
✓ Continuous mode intelligently pauses when you leave the tab to save resources
✓ Integrated with translation history and respects daily usage limits
✓ Live conversation preferences persisted via LocalStorage
✓ Graceful error handling for microphone access and unsupported browsers
✓ Fully responsive — the mic zone scales beautifully on mobile screens
✓ Ukrainian-first interface that stays warm, clear, and friendly throughout
