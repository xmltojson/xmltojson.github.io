# 🎬 Exciting Launch: Movie Watchlist App — Now Live on Base44!

I'm thrilled to announce the release of **Movie Watchlist** — a modern, full-featured film tracking application built and hosted entirely on **Base44**.

Whether you're a casual viewer or a dedicated cinephile, this app gives you everything you need to organize your movie life in one beautiful place.

---

## 🚀 What It Does

**Movie Watchlist** helps you track, rate, review, and organize your favorite films with powerful tools that go far beyond a simple list:

🎥 **Full Movie Management** — Add films with title, year, director, genres, ratings, reviews, and watch status

⭐ **Interactive Ratings** — Rate movies on a 1–5 star scale right from the card

📂 **Smart Playlists** — Create custom playlists or let the app auto-generate collections based on your top-rated films, favorite genres, directors, decades, and more

🔍 **Powerful Search & Filters** — Find any movie instantly with real-time search, genre filtering, and multiple sort options

🌍 **Multilingual** — Full Ukrainian and English language support with instant switching

📊 **Dashboard Stats** — See your total collection, watch progress, average ratings, and playlist count at a glance

---

## 🔐 Built for Real Users

This isn't just a frontend demo. Movie Watchlist is a production-ready application with:

- ☁️ **Cloud Storage** — Your movies, playlists, ratings, and reviews are securely stored in the cloud and accessible from any device
- 🔑 **Authentication** — Sign in to keep your collection private and synced across sessions
- ⚡ **Instant Access** — No installation needed, just open and start tracking
