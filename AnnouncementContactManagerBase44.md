🚀 **Exciting Launch: Contact Manager on Base44!**

I'm thrilled to announce the release of **Contact Manager** — a modern, full-featured contact management application built and hosted on the **Base44** platform.

---

### 🎯 What is Contact Manager?

A sleek, intuitive web application designed to help professionals, teams, and small businesses organize their contacts efficiently — all from a single, beautifully crafted interface.

---

### ✨ Key Features:

📇 **Smart Contact Management**
Create, edit, and delete contacts with rich details — names, phone numbers, emails, notes, and more.

📁 **Custom Groups with Color Coding**
Organize contacts into custom groups with personalized colors for instant visual recognition.

🔍 **Instant Search & Filtering**
Find any contact in milliseconds. Filter by group or search across all fields simultaneously.

🔐 **Secure Authorization**
User authentication ensures your data is private and accessible only to you.

☁️ **Server-Side Data Storage**
All contacts are securely stored on the server — access your data from any device, anytime.

🌙☀️ **Light & Dark Themes**
Work comfortably day or night with a beautiful theme switcher.

🌍 **Multilingual Support**
Full Ukrainian and English interface — switch languages on the fly.

📤 **Flexible Export Options**
Export your contacts to **JSON** for backup or **PDF** for professional reports — with full Cyrillic support.

📥 **Easy Import**
Migrate your existing contacts effortlessly with JSON import.

📱 **Fully Responsive**
Works flawlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
