We're thrilled to present our **Interactive Fireworks Show** —
a mesmerizing canvas-based fireworks simulation that brings the magic of a real pyrotechnic display right to your screen. Click anywhere and watch the night sky come alive!

🎁 WHAT'S INSIDE:

🎆 **10 Explosion Patterns** — Circle, Heart, Star, Ring, Double, Willow, Chrysanthemum, Palm, Spiral & Random
🎨 **10 Color Schemes** — Rainbow, Red, Blue, Green, Gold, Purple, Pastel, Fire, Ice & White
✨ **Glitter Trails** — Sparkling secondary particles that shimmer and fade
💥 **Multi Shot & Finale** — Launch bursts of 3–6 rockets or trigger a spectacular grand finale
🔄 **Auto Mode** — Sit back and enjoy a continuous, self-running show
🔊 **Procedural Sound FX** — Rising launch whistles & booming explosions via Web Audio API
💫 **Radial Screen Flash** — Soft atmospheric light bloom on every detonation
🏙️ **City Skyline** — Glowing window silhouette beneath a starry night sky
⭐ **160 Twinkling Stars** — Organically pulsing background for perfect immersion
⚙️ **Deep Customization** — Tune particle count, explosion size, gravity, trails & speed

🖥️ TECHNICAL HIGHLIGHTS:

✓ Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript — Zero dependencies
✓ Canvas-based rendering with high-DPI (DPR) scaling
✓ Frame-rate-independent delta-time physics — identical speed on any display
✓ **120Hz / 144Hz optimized** with clamped time-stepping
✓ Opaque canvas context & additive-blend compositing for maximum speed
✓ Zero-allocation swap-remove particle management
✓ Auto-pause rendering when the tab is hidden (battery-friendly)
✓ Debounced localStorage for smooth, persistent settings
✓ Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
✓ Glassmorphism UI with a centered, topmost settings popup
